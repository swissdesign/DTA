DTA KIDS

This isn't just another ski club. This is the starting line for the next generation of team skiers.

The Mission

Forget what you know about ski racing. This is a team sport. We're building a crew of dedicated young skiers (ages 10-15) with one ambitious goal: to compete at the European Synchronised Skiing Championships. Our focus is on creating a solid, versatile skiing foundation—covering everything from backcountry skills to race technique—because synchronized skiing demands trust, concentration, and absolute commitment to the team. It's tough, it requires focus, but the reward is a level of skill and camaraderie you won't find anywhere else.
